About Us & Our Values

We’re a small, distributed team that lives and breathes agile & scrum. Short iterations, rapid feedback, and a relentless focus on value keep us shipping features that matter.

Our mission is simple: put people back in control of their data while unlocking the magic of real‑time, real‑world discovery. We believe technology should empower human connection—not harvest it.

Every line of code we write follows three guiding principles:

  • Human‑First AI: AI should respect agency, context, and consent.
  • Data Ownership: Users own their data. Period.
  • Transparent Collaboration: We build in the open and iterate with our community.

Several of our upcoming projects are currently in legal review. Stay tuned as we navigate the formalities and bring even more groundbreaking features to life.
