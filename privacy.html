How We Handle Your Data

We believe personal data should remain personal. Here’s our commitment:

  • No resale of personal data. We never sell, rent, or trade your information.
  • No cookies or tracking scripts without opt‑in. You decide what, if anything, gets stored.
  • User‑owned AI output. Anything our tools generate for you belongs to you, unless we’ve agreed otherwise in a paid collaboration.
  • Encryption in transit & at rest. All data is encrypted via industry‑standard protocols.
  • Minimal retention. We only keep what’s necessary to deliver the service you requested.

If you have any questions, contact us at privacy@ostlii.com.
